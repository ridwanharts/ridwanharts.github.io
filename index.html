Mission & Principles

We design with developmental science, play, and privacy in mind. Our north star: short daily learning that kids love and parents trust.

Safe by design

Large tap targets, calm feedback, no external links in kid mode, and a parental gate for settings and purchases.

Playful pedagogy

Mini‑games under three minutes keep attention high while building core skills in phonics, numeracy, and motor control.

Privacy first
